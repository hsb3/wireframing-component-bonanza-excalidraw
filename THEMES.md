# Theme Comparison

## Available Themes

### default - Grayscale Wireframe
**Use Case:** General wireframing, low-fidelity mockups  
**Style:** Hand-drawn, sketchy  
**Colors:** Pure grayscale (black/white/grays)  
**Font:** Virgil (hand-drawn)  
**Corners:** Rounded (type 3)  
**Roughness:** 1 (sketchy)  

### carbon - IBM Carbon Design System
**Use Case:** Enterprise apps, developer tools, corporate UIs  
**Style:** Clean, technical, high-contrast dark mode  
**Colors:** Dark background (#161616) with IBM Blue (#0f62fe)  
**Font:** Cascadia (monospace)  
**Corners:** Sharp/Boxy (type 1)  
**Roughness:** 0 (clean, no sketch)  
**Contrast:** High (#f4f4f4 text on dark, #c6c6c6 for muted)  

### warm - Earthy Consumer
**Use Case:** Consumer apps, friendly interfaces, approachable designs  
**Style:** Organic, warm, inviting  
**Colors:** Beiges/browns (#fefcf9 bg, #8b4513 primary)  
**Font:** Virgil (hand-drawn)  
**Corners:** Rounded (type 3)  
**Roughness:** 2 (very organic/sketchy)  

## Quick Comparison

| Aspect | default | carbon | warm |
|--------|---------|--------|------|
| Background | White | Very Dark Gray | Warm White |
| Primary | Black | IBM Blue | Brown |
| Font | Hand-drawn | Monospace | Hand-drawn |
| Corners | Rounded | Sharp/Boxy | Rounded |
| Feel | Neutral/Sketchy | Technical/Clean | Friendly/Warm |

## Semantic Tokens

All three themes define the same semantic tokens for consistent theming:
- SUCCESS (green tones)
- INFO (blue tones)  
- WARNING (yellow/amber)
- DESTRUCTIVE (red)
- Plus extended grays and borders

This ensures components like status badges, alerts, and links look appropriate in each theme.
